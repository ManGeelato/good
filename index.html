about us

Our old are the pillar of knowledge, strength and guidance

Goodreef Retirement Village was founded in 2001, dedicated to creating a warm, supportive, and secure environment where seniors can enjoy a fulfilling and comfortable lifestyle in their golden years.

Goodreef Retirement Village is proud to be 100% Black-owned, with a strong foundation of excellence and commitment. We are a LEVEL 1 B-BBEE contributor and an empowering entity in accordance with the DTI's Code of Good Practice, dedicated to fostering an inclusive and supportive community for our residents.

Accountability, Integrity and Transparency

At Goodreef Retirement Village, we believe that thoughtful design and comfortable living spaces can create meaningful change in the lives of our residents. That’s why we focus on creating an environment that fosters connection, comfort, and a sense of belonging for everyone who calls our village home.

Goodreef Retirement Village is proud to have built a thriving community, serving hundreds of residents over the years. We continue to partner with trusted names to ensure our facilities and services meet the highest standards of comfort, care, and quality for our residents.

At Goodreef Retirement Village, we embrace our responsibility to make our residents' experience exceptional. From the initial stages of settling in to ensuring all their needs are met with care and efficiency, we are with them every step of the way.
This dedication has resulted in a strong community, built on trust, referrals, and long-lasting relationships that we deeply value.
